About Our University


University of Mumbai, informally known as Mumbai University (MU) is one of the oldest state universities in India. Mumbai University was established in 1857. The University of Mumbai is ranked 61 in the 'University' category by the NIRF 2024. Mumbai University is approved by the UGC and accredited with an A++ grade by NAAC. Internationally, the University of Mumbai was ranked 711-720 by the QS World University Rankings 2025 and 245 by the QS Asia Rankings 2025.

MU has two campuses Vidyanagri & Fort and three sub-campuses at Ratnagiri, Thane, and Kalyan. Mumbai University offers over 200 UG, PG, diploma, doctoral and certificate courses across various disciplines, such as Engineering, Arts, Science, Management, Law, etc. through 56 departments and over 700 affiliated colleges. The University of Mumbai also offers distance learning programmes from its Institute of Distance and Open Learning (IDOL Mumbai University). University of Mumbai course admission is based on merit or entrance exam.
